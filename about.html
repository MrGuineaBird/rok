About Me

Building ROK as a private local AI workspace

I built ROK because I wanted an AI setup that feels like a real workspace, not a generic chat clone. The goal is fast iteration, local-first control, and a cleaner place to build ideas, code, and writing.

Why ROK Exists

Most AI tools prioritize polished chat marketing over practical workflow control. ROK is meant to be simple, direct, and yours. You decide how it runs, where it points, and what it stores.

Current Focus

  • Workspace-first user experience.
  • Reliable local + proxy runtime behavior.
  • Clear docs and practical onboarding.
  • Quality improvements based on real usage.

Long-Term Vision

Turn ROK into a serious personal AI workstation where local-first control and strong UX are the baseline. Fewer gimmicks, more useful work.
